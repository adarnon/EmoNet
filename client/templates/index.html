EmoNet

Help train a machine learning model to understand human emotions!

Instructions

  1. 1. Use the Record button to record yourself saying something in the prompted emotion.
  2. 2. Hit the Stop button when you're done.
  3. 3. Listen to your recording and click Record if you want to try again.
  4. 4. Click Predict if you want to see how the current model guessed your emotion.
  5. 5. Click Submit when you're done and we will anonymously improve our model while keeping your data on your local device.
